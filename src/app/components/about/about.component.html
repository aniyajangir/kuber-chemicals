Kuber Chemicals

We, Kuber Chemicals are a reputed name, which is engaged in manufacturing, supplying, distributing, retailing and wholesaling a wide range of Chemicals, primaraly Lime products. Under this range, we offer clients Industrial Chemicals, Agriculture Chemicals and Dyeing Chemicals.

Utilizing one of the highest purity limestone reserves available, Kuber Chemicals has become the most diversified producer of high calcium lime in India with mining facilitity in Nagaur of Rajasthan. The deposits has proven mineral reserves of more than 8 million tons. The mineral is characterized by a high degree of purity, low iron oxide content and almost no heavy metals. With a commitment to research and development, Kuber Chemicals is setting itself up to ensure that it can continue its exponential growth, focusing in part on latest technology & qualitative needs. We are a process driven and detail oriented organization. We believe in transparency, ownership and accountability at all the levels of any job undertaken.To ensure product quality, we take advantage of the latest in process control methods such as Statistical Process Control (SPC) and Statistical Quality Control (SQC) techniques.vasudev chemical plant is Quality of ISO 9001:2008 quality assurance procedures and follow the required standards.

We formulate these chemicals using best quality ingredients in accurate composition. Offered chemicals are known among the clients for their accurate composition, longer shelf life and environment friendliness. These chemicals are commonly used in the various industries such as agriculture, manufacturing, construction ,paper, paint and pharma. We also provide commissoning services to our valuable clients.

We have developed a sophisticated infrastructure facility that aids us in offering pure chemicals to the clients. Our labs are will equipped with the required instruments and facilities for the smooth execution.
