Kuber Chemicals

We, Kuber Chemicals are a reputed name, which is engaged in manufacturing, supplying, distributing, retailing and wholesaling a wide range of Chemicals, primaraly Lime products. Under this range, we offer clients Industrial Chemicals, Agriculture Chemicals and Dyeing Chemicals.

Utilizing one of the highest purity limestone reserves available, Kuber Chemicals has become the most diversified producer of high calcium lime in India with mining facilitity in Nagaur of Rajasthan. The deposits has proven mineral reserves of more than 8 million tons. The mineral is characterized by a high degree of purity, low iron oxide content and almost no heavy metals.
